About

I’m interested in the space where physical signals meet imperfect systems—and somehow still need to make sense.

I like building things that start messy: noisy sensor data, unstable connections, incomplete information—and working through them until the system becomes predictable and usable.

Recently, I’ve been exploring how machine learning can assist in this process, especially in monitoring and anomaly detection, while also focusing on building simple, usable interfaces that help people respond to what the system sees.
